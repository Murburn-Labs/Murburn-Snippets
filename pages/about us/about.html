Dr. K Murali Manoj

Meet Dr. Kelath Murali Manoj, a Professor at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore. He leverages his expertise in artificial intelligence to explore and understand the mechanisms underlying life-sustaining and disruptive cellular processes, particularly within the framework of the Murburn concept.

Research Interests:

  • Murburn concept and its applications
  • Cellular bioenergetics
  • Artificial intelligence in biological research
