Murzyme Analysis Plots

A comprehensive analysis of murzymes based on data from 500+ PDB files with extracted features. The visualizations below show various aspects of murzyme classification and characteristics.

Principal Component Analysis

The PCA plot shows dimensionality reduction of 384-length semantic vectors, allowing visualization of patterns distinguishing murzymes from non-murzymes.

Feature Importance

This bar chart shows the importance of different features in classifying murzymes vs non-murzymes.

Binding Energy Distribution

Histogram showing the distribution of binding energies in murzymes compared to non-murzymes.

Classification Performance

Bar chart showing the performance metrics of the murzyme classification model.
